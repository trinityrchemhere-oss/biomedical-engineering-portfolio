Advanced Imaging & Diagnostics

Engineers design and improve imaging systems—mammography, tomosynthesis (3D mammography), MRI, PET/CT—and the computational methods that analyze images (AI-based image analysis, radiomics) to detect cancer earlier and more accurately.

Mammography & Tomosynthesis

Improved detectors and reconstruction algorithms reduce false positives and improve lesion visibility in dense breast tissue.

Image Analysis (AI)

Machine learning models assist radiologists by highlighting suspicious regions and quantifying tumor characteristics.
