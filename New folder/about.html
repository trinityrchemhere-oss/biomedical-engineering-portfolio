About this page

This brief educational site was created to illustrate how biomedical engineering contributes to breast cancer care — covering imaging, targeted therapies, surgical technology, and reconstruction. Content is introductory and intended for students and the general public; it does not replace professional medical guidance.

About the author

My name is Trinity Ropafadzo Chemhere. I am a first-year Biomedical Engineering student at the Faculty of Medicine and Health Sciences, University of Zimbabwe. This site is a learning project and portfolio documenting examples of biomedical engineering approaches to breast cancer diagnosis, treatment, and reconstruction.
