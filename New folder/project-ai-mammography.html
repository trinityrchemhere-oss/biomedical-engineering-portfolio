Overview

This prototype uses a convolutional neural network trained on public mammography datasets to produce heatmaps indicating regions that may warrant further clinical review. The pipeline includes pre-processing, model inference and a small UI to visualise probability maps alongside original images.

Tech

Python, PyTorch, OpenCV, Flask (visualiser)

Notes

Data used for research purposes only. This is not a diagnostic device.
